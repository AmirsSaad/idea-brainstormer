# Drone Video Relay Add-On with Beam-Tracking Antenna
Date: 2026-05-23

## Summary
A relay add-on module for drones that receives video at 5.8 GHz and retransmits at 1.3 GHz. Key feature is beam-tracking capability using two RX antennas: a omnidirectional "lollypop" antenna and a directional patch antenna mounted on a PTZ mechanism for active beam steering.
